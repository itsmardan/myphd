Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to myPhD.org. Protecting your privacy and your personal data is of paramount importance to us. As a consultancy firm specialsing in facilitating PhD enrollments within the United Kingdom, we are committed to handling your information with the highest degree of care and confidentiality.

Use of Information

The personal information we collect is exclusively used to assist you in securing PhD enrollment opportunities in the UK. We tailor our services to your individual needs, ensuring the data we gather is directly relevant to the academic aspirations we help you achieve.

Data Confidentiality and Security

At myPhD.org, we place your data’s security and confidentiality at the forefront of our operations. We employ state-of-the-art security measures, including advanced encryption and firewall technologies, to protect your information from unauthorised access and ensure its safety.

Data Collection Limitations

Our philosophy is to collect only the information essential for providing you with our bespoke consultancy services. We avoid storing sensitive personal data unnecessarily, adhering strictly to data minimisation principles in our information gathering practices.

World-Class Data Security Measures

We are steadfast in our commitment to maintaining the highest standards of data security. Leveraging the latest in cybersecurity technology, we ensure your personal information is safeguarded within our secure digital infrastructure.

Data Retention Policy

Our data retention policy is designed to hold your information for only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected. Sensitive information is not retained longer than needed, and we prefer secure, digital methods for any transactions to minimise data storage.

User Rights

In compliance with the GDPR and the UK Data Protection Act, you have rights over your data, including access, correction, and deletion. We support your right to control your personal information and welcome any requests related to your data rights.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. The most up-to-date version will always be accessible on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to remain informed about how we protect your information.
